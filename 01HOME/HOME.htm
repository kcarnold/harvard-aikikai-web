Harvard Aikikai is a student organization dedicated to teaching members of the Harvard community (students, faculty, staff, and alumni) about the principles and techniques of Aikido.

All are welcome; come visit a class (see the schedule)! You could watch, but it's more fun to participate; just wear comfortable work-out clothing and bring a registration form.

To stay informed, sign up for our announcements-only mailing list.

Harvard Aikikai is affiliated with the United States Aikido Federation.
